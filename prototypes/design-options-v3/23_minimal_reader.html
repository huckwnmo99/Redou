Minimal reader mode

Make the paper feel like the product, not the chrome around it.

Minimal Reader is the least “app-like” option here. The interface gets out of the way and treats the paper itself as the central canvas.

Folders, notes, and figures still exist, but they stay peripheral until called in. This is the cleanest option if you want calm more than control panels.

Use when long reading sessions matter most.
